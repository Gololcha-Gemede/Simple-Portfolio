About Me

Introduction

I am Gololcha Gemede Ermias. I was born in Bule Hora,Oromia,Ethipia.I have three sisters and one brother.I am the second child of my parents,younger than my elder sister. As a child I was a quiet and shy boy.So, I did not spend much of my childhood playing with friends as most children would do. My father, who is a teacher, used to work in a rural village in Bule Hora woreda. As a result we used to live with him there outside of Bule Hora town. But when I was 3, we moved to Bule Hora town so me and my siblings would go to a better school.

Education

At the age of five ,I went to Sinaye Academy and learnt all of my elementary education there. After completing and passing the Regional exam I joined Bule Hora high school. After completing grade 10, I took the National Matric exam and scored 4.0/4.0 and then joined Bule Hora preparatory school. After completing the 12th grade I took the National secondary education leaving exam and scored 588/700. I then joined joined Addis Ababa University to pursue my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.I am currently a 4th year student and my expected year of graduation is July 2025.
